Jorge Garcia-Pueyo

I'm a PhD candidate in Computer Graphics and Computational Imaging at Graphics and Imaging Lab (University of Zaragoza), supervised by Adolfo Muñoz. My research explores the physics and applications of light, including advanced light transport simulation, techniques for imaging around corners (non-line-of-sight) and novel touch sensors based on the time-of-flight of the light. I am also interested in problems related to Computer Vision.

I am always eager to connect, exchange ideas, and keep learning. If you like something I do, just send an email!
