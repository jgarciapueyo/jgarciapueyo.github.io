# Hello,

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Graphics and Computational Imaging with focus on Transient Imaging at [Graphics and Imaging Lab (Universidad de Zaragoza)](https://graphics.unizar.es), supervised by [Adolfo Muñoz](http://webdiis.unizar.es/~amunoz/). My main research interests are light transport, including time-resolved light transport simulation, and computational imaging, especially for imaging around corners (non-line-of-sight). I am also interested in problems related to computer vision.

Previously, I received a BSc in Computer Science (2021) and an MSc in Robotics, Graphics, and Computer Vision (2024).
